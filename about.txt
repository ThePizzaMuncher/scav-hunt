# Welcome to Scavenger Hunt project!

## What is Scavenger Hunt?

Scavenger Hunt is an exciting adventure game where you embark on a quest. You will follow clues, see nice sity, and explore fascinating locations to discover some cool stuff.

## How to Play

To join the adventure, gather your friends or form a team and register on our website. Once registered, you'll receive a unique command code leading you to various checkpoints. Follow the clues, explore the surroundings, and collect information at each checkpoint.

## Win Amazing Rewards

The more checkpoints you visit, the closer you get to the grand prize. Compete with other players or teams to earn the highest score and win something nice.
